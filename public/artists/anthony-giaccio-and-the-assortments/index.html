Anthony Giaccio & The Assortments

Anthony Giaccio & The Assortments

Anthony Giaccio has been writing songs most of his adult life, but only recently started recording and performing them.  He has recorded two albums, Out Run the Rain and recently released Down This Highway. Both albums feature his band The Assortments.  His songs can be described as being a little bit of country and a little bit of Rock and Roll.  This mix creates a unique but familiar sound that has been well received by listeners.  Giaccio and The Assortments perform at various venues in Westchester and New York City.
